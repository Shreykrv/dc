Satya Narayan Vrat Katha

Satya Narayan Vrat Katha is a popular Hindu religious observance dedicated to Lord Vishnu, specifically in his manifestation as Satya Narayan, the embodiment of truth. The vrat (vow) is typically observed on the Purnima (full moon) day of any month, though it can also be performed on Ekadashi or Sankranti days. It is believed that by observing this vrat with devotion, devotees can attain divine blessings, prosperity, and fulfillment of desires.
